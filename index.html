Wiktopher is a fiction story taking place in a fantasy world, consisting of roughly 60,000 words. The novel will be in black and white, and have an illustrated glossary, a language alphabet (with songs) a world map and recipes. All of these, will permit the reader to learn everything about the culture of each place visited. A noteworthy aspect of the story, is that it’s told without the use of gender pronouns.

Fauna and Flora

The glossary will contain all of the (fictional) plants and animals mentioned in the text. It will also have recipes concocted with these plants. The recipes will appear when there is mention of cooking (with cooking instructions). These glossaries will serve to develop the world and intricacies of each region. In the text, there will be notes on the whistled language “ilken”. The reader will be able to learn this language, with the instructions provided in the text. The book will be also feature an illustrated world map, denoting each region mentioned in the text.
